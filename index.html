About SAMRA Leathers

SAMRA as a company was founded in 2012. The company stands for originality and uncompromising designs. SAMRA uses international standard premium quality leather. Most of the leather is made as per the specifications and requirements of SAMRA and only for SAMRA. SAMRA is an eco-friendly company, leaving no waste products to be dumped, as scraps will be used as an input for small accessories and floor mats. SAMRA is established in a belief of producing high qualities of clarity, flexibility, strength, thickness and compact texture. As 80 percent of the employees are women, SAMRA aims to contribute for the growth of the economy through women’s empowerment. High-End Luxury and Sophisticated design is what sets apart SAMRA.
